STRIDE Study: Remepan Shines with Superior Efficacy

Exciting new data from the STRIDE study showcases the remarkable efficacy of Remepan. The study, involving a diverse population of 5,000 patients with type 2 diabetes, revealed that Remepan outperformed other treatments in reducing HbA1c levels by an average of 1.5%. These results confirm the outstanding efficacy of Remepan in achieving optimal glycemic control.

In the STRIDE study, Remepan demonstrated its superiority across multiple parameters, including reducing fasting plasma glucose and improving postprandial glucose levels. These findings provide healthcare providers with a compelling reason to consider Remepan as a first-line treatment for their patients with type 2 diabetes.
